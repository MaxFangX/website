We bring cryptocurrencies to the East Bay.

We're a student organization at UC Berkeley dedicated to connecting the Berkeley and greater East Bay communities to the crypto ecosystem. Our members include Berkeley students, alumni, community members, and Bitcoiners from all educational and industrial backgrounds.

Our Goals

Education

We organize tech talks, meetings, and conduct research on everything from social effect to the nitty gritty details of new-and-upcoming cryptocurrency technology to allow everyone to become part of Bitcoin history.

Advocacy

Bitcoin allows merchants to easily accept payments without transaction fees. We work with industry leaders and local businesses to integrate cryptocurrencies as a payment method.

Community

The Bitcoin Association of Berkeley consists of students, alumni, community members, and others from all fields of study, including Computer Science, Economics, and the Liberal Arts. We are an open discussion space for anything crypto.
